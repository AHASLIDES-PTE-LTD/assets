1. Information Collection and Usage

Our primary goal in collecting information is to provide you with a seamless and secure VPN experience. Here is a detailed breakdown of the types of information we collect and how we utilize them:

Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather information about how you interact with our website and Services. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. Web beacons (also known as pixel tags or clear GIFs) are small graphic images embedded in web pages or emails that help us analyze website usage and email effectiveness. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

 

Customer Support: When you contact our customer support team, we may collect additional information such as your contact details and the content of your communications with us. We use this information to assist you with your inquiries and improve our customer support services.

 

Surveys and Promotions: From time to time, we may invite you to participate in surveys, contests, or promotions. Participation in these activities is voluntary, and any information you provide may be used for the purpose of administering the activity and communicating with you about it.
